Quick Takeaways on Investment Time Horizons . ·        Time horizons are periods where investments are held until they are needed. ·        Time horizons vary according to the investment goal, short or long. ·        Time horizons also vary according to the time by which you begin investing. ·        The longer the longer the time horizon, the longer the power of compounding has to work. ·        Generally speaking, the longer the time horizon, the more aggressive an investor can be in their portfolio, and vice versa.
