American options allow investors to realize a profit as soon as the stock price moves in their favor and enough to more than offset the premium paid. Comparing European Options to American Options . Investors will use American options with dividend-paying stocks. In this way, they can exercise the option before an ex-dividend date. The ex-date is the day by which investors need to own shares of a company's stock to receive the dividend payment. A dividend is a payment in either cash or stock to shareholders of record by the ex-date. The flexibility of American options allows investors to own a company's shares in time to get paid a dividend.
