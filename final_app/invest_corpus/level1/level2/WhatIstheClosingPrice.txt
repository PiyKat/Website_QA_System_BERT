 What Is the Closing Price? . Even in the era of 24-hour trading, there is a closing price for any stock or other security, and it is the final price at which it trades during regular market hours on any given day. The closing price is considered the most accurate valuation of a stock or other security until trading resumes on the next trading day. What Is the Closing Price? . Most stocks and other financial instruments are traded after-hours, although in far smaller volumes. Therefore, the closing price of any security is often different from its after-hours price.
