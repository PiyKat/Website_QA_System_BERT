What Does Negative Shareholders' Equity Mean? . Shareholders' equity, which is listed on the balance sheet, is used by investors to determine the financial health of a company. Shareholders' equity represents the amount that would be returned to shareholders if all the company's assets were liquidated and all its debts repaid. In short, shareholders' equity measures the company's net worth.What Does Negative Shareholders' Equity Mean? .
