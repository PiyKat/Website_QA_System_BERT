 Understanding the GBP . The British pound has one of the highest trading volumes in the world, trailing only the U.S. dollar, euro, and Japanese yen in daily volume. The British pound accounts for roughly 13% of the daily trading volume in foreign exchange markets. The pound symbol is £, while the euro symbol is €. Understanding the GBP . The most common currency pairs involving the British pound are the euro (EUR/GBP) and the U.S. dollar (GBP/USD). GBP/USD is referred to as "cable" by foreign exchange traders.
