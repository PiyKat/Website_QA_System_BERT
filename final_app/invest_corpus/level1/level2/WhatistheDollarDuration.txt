 What is the Dollar Duration . The dollar duration measures the dollar change in a bond's value to a change in the market interest rate. The dollar duration is used by professional bond fund managers as a way of approximating the portfolio's interest rate risk. Dollar duration is one of the several different measurements of bond duration. What is the Dollar Duration .
