 How to Use Variance . Variance measures variability from the average or mean. To investors, variability is volatility, and volatility is a measure of risk. Therefore, the variance statistic can help determine the risk an investor assumes when purchasing a specific security. How to Use Variance . A large variance indicates that numbers in the set are far from the mean and from each other, while a small variance indicates the opposite. How to Use Variance . Variance can be negative. A variance value of zero indicates that all values within a set of numbers are identical. How to Use Variance . All variances that are not zero will be positive numbers.
