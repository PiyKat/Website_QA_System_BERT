 Lambda in Action . If a share of stock for ABC trades at $40 in April and a MAY 45 call sells for $2. The option's lambda is 0.15 and volatility is 20%. Lambda in Action . If the underlying volatility increased by 1% to 21%, then theoretically, the option price should move higher to $2 + (1 x 0.15) = $2.15. Lambda in Action . Alternatively, if volatility declined by 3% to 17% instead, then the option should fall to $2 - (3 x 0.15) = $1.55
