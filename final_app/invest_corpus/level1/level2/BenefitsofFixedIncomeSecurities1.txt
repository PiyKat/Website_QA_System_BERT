 Benefits of Fixed-Income Securities . Fixed-income securities are rated by credit rating agencies allowing investors to choose bonds from financially-stable issuers Benefits of Fixed-Income Securities . Although stock prices can fluctuate wildly over time, fixed-income securities usually have less price volatility risk Benefits of Fixed-Income Securities . Fixed-income securities such as U.S. Treasuries are guaranteed by the government providing a safe return for investors Benefits of Fixed-Income Securities . Fixed-income securities have credit risk meaning the issuer can default on making the interest payments or paying back the principal Benefits of Fixed-Income Securities . Fixed-income securities typically pay a lower rate of return than other investments such as equities Benefits of Fixed-Income Securities . Inflation risk can be an issue if prices rise by a faster rate than the interest rate on the fixed-income security Benefits of Fixed-Income Securities .
