 What Is Proprietary Technology? . Proprietary technology is any combination of processes, tools, or systems of interrelated connections that are the property of a business or individual. These combinations provide a benefit or competitive advantage to the owners of proprietary technologies. What Is Proprietary Technology? . Companies capable of developing useful proprietary technologies in-house are rewarded with a valuable asset and can either use it exclusively or profit from the sale of licensing their technology to other parties. What Is Proprietary Technology? . Access to valuable proprietary technologies can also be purchased. This option, however, is often costlier and comes with greater restrictions on the use of underlying technologies.
